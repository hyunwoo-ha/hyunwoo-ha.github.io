Hyunwoo Ha

I am a Ph.D. student in Electrical Engineering at POSTECH, advised by Prof. Tae-Hyun Oh. I work with the Advanced Machine Intelligence Lab at KAIST.

My research interests lie in physical AI and visual intelligence, including motion understanding, robust perception, 3D reconstruction, and multimodal learning.
